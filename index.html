LASERS
■ Red lasers — low beams. Jump over them (press Space).
■ Blue lasers — high beams. Crouch under them (hold Shift).
Triggering a laser starts the escape countdown — get out fast!
SECURITY
Security cameras sweep set arcs. Crouch to avoid detection.
Guards patrol routes — distract, avoid, or take them down.
Hack terminals to loop cameras for 20 seconds.
Cut the breaker to half-blind guards for 30 seconds.
OBJECTIVES
1. Enter the Louvre through the front gate.
2. Find the Yellow Keycard and reach the Gallery.
3. Steal La Joconde from the west gallery wall.
4. Find the Blue Keycard and reach the Crown Vault.
5. Crack the safe and steal the Crown.
6. Escape via the service exit or front gate.
